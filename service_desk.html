Benefits of Service Desk

This setup allows us to keep you updated at every stage, providing a seamless experience and clear communication.

  • Issue Sharing: Share ticket with third parties or colleagues to solve the customer issue faster. No costs incurred or loss of visibility or access to it.
  • Automated Email Notification: Instant email notification of closed/resolved issues,remarks and feedback.
  • Inbox Management: The Service Desk acts as a centralized platform, where all inquiries are categorized and prioritized. This structure not only minimizes response time but also allows us to manage high volumes of requests effectively.
  • Assignment Collision: Automated assignment of issues to ensure multiple agents are not working on the same issue.
  • Efficiency: No app-switching. Discuss issues raised within its context.
  • Secure by Nature: People invited to see a ticket cannot access any other Ticket.
  • Automated Ticket Dispatch: Default assignment of issues to agents. Instant Email notification of dispatched ticket.
  • Report Generation: Rating of the services by the customer.
  • Customer Feedback Ratings: Generate excel reports of issues depending on their status. Further analysis can be done.
