About our Survey Service

SMS-Survey is a product that enables every organization to get feedback from the customer concerning any survey that they want to undertake. It is a forward way of transmitting messages to and from mobiles. SMS-Survey is a product enhanced in providing interactive platform system to any organization

Get Service

How it works

An account purposely for this product will be set to the organization, then the client will be logging in to the portal to view the automatically responded received SMS and respond to the open SMS tasks.When they want to view and respond to code SMS one can simply log in to our SMS portal using a user name and a password that has been provided. The organization sends the bulk SMS to the target audience group and the recipients replies to the short code texts.

Service Benefits

Benefits of survey

  • Fast and Convenient Feedback Collection: SMS surveys allow you to quickly gather responses from customers, employees, or stakeholders, making it easy for them to engage anytime and anywhere.
  • High Response Rates: With SMS having a near-instant open rate, your surveys are more likely to be seen and answered compared to other communication methods like email.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Conducting surveys via SMS is affordable, eliminating the need for printed forms or extensive software while reaching a wider audience.
  • Real-Time Insights: Gather data in real-time, enabling you to make timely and informed decisions for your business or organization.
  • Improved Customer Engagement: SMS surveys create a direct and personal communication channel, helping you build stronger relationships with your audience.
  • Wide Reach: SMS surveys work on any mobile device, ensuring that you can connect with users even in remote areas without internet access.
  • Customizable and Scalable: Tailor your surveys to fit specific needs, whether you’re reaching a small focus group or conducting large-scale data collection.

Requirements for service:

  • Authorization letter for short code creation – should be signed and stamped and in letter head
  • Setup payments
  • Waiting period of two weeks to set up.
